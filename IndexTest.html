Welcome Minecrafters!

Washington County Library now has a Minecraft server. Our goal is to provide a safe, friendly,community environment for Minecraft players to work together, build, and have fun. All you need to get started is a Minecraft account and a library card.

About our Server

Washington County Library has a great server running multiple worlds in survival. The purpose of survival is to challenge you a little bit while giving you a fun place to come back to over and over. No need to carry around your flash drives anymore!

In the near future Washington County Library plans to have regularly scheduled events where you will have access to a creative world to build. Also coming soon are mini-games and new worlds to explore.
